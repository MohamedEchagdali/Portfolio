About us

Our Mission and Impact

At Furry Friends Rescue, we are dedicated to protecting and caring for animals in need, both domestic and wild. Our mission is to provide safe shelter, medical care, and loving homes for abandoned and abused animals, including wildlife. From rescuing injured birds to rehabilitating orphaned wildlife, our team is committed to ensuring every animal receives the care they deserve. Your support is crucial in helping us continue our mission. By donating, you are directly contributing to the well-being and future of countless animals, domestic and wild alike. Together, we can make a lasting impact on their lives.
